> initial delivery. Key changes reflected below:
> - `HabitsRoute` has been **removed**. `HomeRoute` now renders `HabitsScreen` directly.

>   to a custom `Row` of `BottomBarTab` composables: active tab = deep-teal rounded-square pill
>   (16dp corners), inactive = transparent at 55% opacity.
> - The `SerializersModule` in `MudawamaAppShell` registers `QuranRoute` instead of `HabitsRoute`.
> - The bottom nav tabs are: **Home, Prayer, Quran, Athkar** (not Home, Prayer, Athkar, Habits).
